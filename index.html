The Crisis: A System at its Breaking Point

  • Caregiver Shortage: Our aging population faces a critical shortage of available care, placing an unsustainable burden on both professional and family caregivers.
  • Unsustainable Costs: Current healthcare models of support are financially unsustainable for long-term home care.
  • Personalized Care Gap: Existing solutions lack 24/7 tailored treatment and continuous in-home observation.

Our Solution: Clinically Governed Home Humanoid Care (HHC)

HHC delivers a safe, compassionate humanoid assistant that operates under clinical oversight. We transform commoditized humanoid hardware into an indispensable, trusted caregiver by providing the essential software layer. This solution supports daily living without replacing human caregivers, instead filling the critical gaps they cannot cover.

HHC Delivers:

  • Medication Adherence: Reminders and adherence support.
  • Safety Monitoring: Continuous observation for falls, wandering, and missed routines.
  • Routine Daily Assistance: Mobility prompts, hydration reminders, and cognitive stimulation.
  • Clinical Bridge: Seamless escalation to physician telepresence via a conversational AI Agent, integrated with EHR.

Safety OS: The Regulatory Moat and Clinical Governance Layer

Our SaMD (Software as a Medical Device) operating system is the critical bridge that ensures the robot operates safely within clinical and ethical boundaries. This is where our strategic value lies.

Safety OS ensures:

  • Human-in-the-Loop Supervision: The Architect (Andy Squire) performs final User Acceptance Testing (UAT). The human remains the pilot (AIITL model).
  • Compliance: Strict adherence to Security Standards for HIPAA/GDPR to protect patient data.
  • Fail-Safe Controls: Action boundaries are defined and governed by the Manus Protocol for robust and compliant technical implementation.
