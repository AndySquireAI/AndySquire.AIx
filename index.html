The Problem: The Global Care Crisis

By 2050, more than 1.6 billion older adults will lack access to a human caregiver. Families are overextended, clinicians lack visibility between visits, and traditional care models cannot meet demand. Patients living with early cognitive decline, chronic conditions, or mobility limitations are especially vulnerable. A scalable, supervised form of intelligent home support is urgently needed.

The Solution: Home Humanoid Care (HHC)

Home Humanoid Care is the first clinically governed, regulatory-aligned framework for deploying humanoid assistance safely inside the home. The purpose is not to replace caregivers but to extend independence, reduce risk, and support families with reliable, supervised assistance at home.

Humanoid support will include:

  • Routine and medication reminders
  • Safety and activity monitoring
  • Mobility prompts & guided rehab routines
  • Cognitive engagement
  • Early detection of decline
  • Rapid escalation to caregivers or clinicians

A Responsible, Evidence-Driven Path to Humanoid Care

Humanoid caregiving must be developed, tested, and validated through rigorous stages, with safety and clinical governance at the center. Our multi-phase roadmap emphasizes evidence first, safety at every stage, collaboration with caregivers and clinicians, and gradual expansion of capabilities. This ensures humanoid support enters the home responsibly, predictably, and ethically.

Timeline Blueprint:

  • Phase 1: Early Insights & Safety Research
  • Phase 2: Clinical Protocol Development
  • Phase 3: Assisted Home Support Models
  • Phase 4: Full Home Humanoid Care (HHC)

Clinical Impact

  • Safety First: Humanoid actions must be predictable and bounded by clinical protocols.
  • Evidence-Driven: Each capability is validated before expansion.
  • Trust & Transparency: Patients and families must understand the system and remain in control.
  • Regulatory Alignment: Safety OS is designed to meet the expectations of health authorities for supervised, non-autonomous assistance.
  • Scalability: This phased approach supports millions of households and the growing demographic crisis.

Safety OS: The Clinical Governance Layer

Safety OS is the foundation that makes humanoid caregiving clinically acceptable and ethically deployable.

Safety OS provides:

  • Human-in-the-loop supervision
  • Regulated behavioral boundaries
  • Risk detection & fail-safes
  • Clear escalation pathways
  • Transparent logging & auditability
  • Privacy-first data handling

Designed for Real Home Needs

  • Early cognitive decline support
  • Post-hospital rehabilitation reinforcement
  • Chronic condition routine management
  • Mobility & safety prompting
  • Night-time monitoring
  • Aging-at-home independence
