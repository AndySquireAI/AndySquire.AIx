Mistake #1: Tool Hopping

Don't buy 10 tools and use none of them well. Master one tool at a time.

Mistake #2: Not Training Your Team

AI tools only save time if people actually use them. Invest in training.

Mistake #3: Expecting 100% Automation

AI assists humans, it doesn't replace them completely (yet). Expect 60-80% automation.

Mistake #4: Ignoring Data Security

Read privacy policies. Don't put sensitive customer data into public AI tools.

Mistake #5: Forgetting to Measure ROI

Track time saved and money saved. If a tool isn't delivering ROI, cancel it.
