Regulatory Snapshot (30-Second Review)

  • All escalation requires explicit user consent

Intended Use Declaration

Declared Non-Clinical Intended Use

  • Phase 1 is designed exclusively for non-clinical social support and engagement
  • No medical purpose is claimed, implied, or enabled
  • No health status, disease state, or cognitive condition is assessed or inferred

Regulatory Posture

Explicit Functional Boundaries

Capabilities Allowed in Phase 1

  • Conversational companionship
  • Social engagement and emotional presence
  • Reminders (non-medical)
  • Check-ins without interpretation or assessment
  • User-initiated requests for human contact

Capabilities Explicitly Prohibited

  • Diagnosis or differential diagnosis
  • Clinical assessment or triage
  • Risk scoring or health inference
  • Cognitive or disease detection
  • Treatment recommendation
  • Autonomous escalation

Decision Authority & Control

No Autonomous Decision-Making

  • AI does not decide, recommend, or prioritize clinical action
  • AI does not determine urgency or severity
  • AI does not act without explicit user instruction

Human Authority is Architecturally Enforced

  • All real-world actions are executed by humans
  • AI cannot bypass caregivers, clinicians, or consent gates
  • Responsibility remains fully with human actors

Escalation & Consent Model

Escalation is User-Initiated Only

  • No system-initiated escalation
  • No inferred distress triggers
  • No background monitoring or alerting

Consent is Explicit and Transactional

  • Each escalation requires affirmative user confirmation
  • Consent is captured per action, not assumed
  • Consent state is logged immutably

Escalation Pathways

  • User ‚Üí AI ‚Üí Human caregiver / doctor's office
  • AI does not interpret, filter, or prioritize escalation content

Data Classification & Use

  • No biometric data

  • No clinical annotations

Permitted Data Use

  • Interaction metadata

Explicit Exclusions

  • No health outcome measurement

Auditability & Evidence

Immutable Audit Logging

  • All interactions logged
  • All consent events recorded
  • All escalations timestamped

Audit Readiness
