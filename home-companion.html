Home Companion

Governance-first AI companionship for seniors aging safely at home

Non-medical, voice-first AI designed to provide companionship, reassurance, and human escalation - never medical advice.

Download Overview View Governance Roadmap

What Home Companion Is

Phase 1: Non-Medical AI

Home Companion is intentionally designed as a Phase 1, non-medical, non-SaMD AI system. It operates with a caregiver-in-the-loop by design, supporting seniors living independently and aging in place.

Every interaction is governed by explicit safety boundaries, ensuring that medical advice is never provided and escalation occurs only with user confirmation.

  • Conversational companionship and reassurance
  • Explicit refusal of medical advice
  • User-confirmed escalation only
  • Multilingual, voice-first interface
  • Always-on audit logging

Why Governance Comes First

Home Companion is intentionally designed outside clinical claims while embedding governance, consent, escalation, and accountability from day one.

  • Safety boundaries enforced at system level
  • No diagnosis, treatment, or clinical decision-making
  • Designed for post-incident reconstructability

Safety OS - "Flight Recorder" Concept

Safety OS Flight Recorder (Illustrative)
  • Every interaction logged
  • Boundary events, refusals, and escalations recorded
  • Immutable, reviewable audit trail
  • Supports caregiver, clinician, and regulator trust

Phased Roadmap

Phase 1 - Home Companion (Today)

  • Non-medical
  • Caregiver-in-the-loop
  • Audit-first deployment

Phase 2 - SaMD-Ready / Physician-as-Pilot

  • Governance elevated to clinical-grade
  • Physician-supervised workflows
  • No autonomous clinical authority

Phase 3 - Regulated SaMD / Embodied Care

  • FDA / EU MDR pathways
  • Modular SaMD plug-ins
  • Physical embodiments inherit proven governance

Capabilities advance only after governance maturity is demonstrated
