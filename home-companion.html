The Independence Gap

Millions of seniors face a growing crisis that existing systems are not designed to address.

Seniors aging alone at scale

14.7 million older adults live alone, many without daily human contact or reliable check-in systems.

Isolation increases health risk

Social isolation raises all-cause mortality risk by 35% and drives avoidable emergency department visits.

Families carry chronic uncertainty

Caregivers lack reliable daily signals about their loved one's wellbeing, creating anxiety and reactive care patterns.

Avoidable escalation costs

Isolated seniors visit emergency departments more often. Early engagement reduces unplanned care costs.

A Safe, Voice-First Companion Designed for Daily Life

Home Companion provides gentle, daily engagement that keeps seniors connected and families informed.

  • Conversational companionship and reassurance
  • Gentle daily check-ins
  • Human-confirmed escalation only
  • No diagnosis, no medical advice
  • Designed explicitly outside clinical claims
